Cookies on contact Ofqual

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to collect and store information about how you use this digital service, such as the pages you visit.

This page has a brief explanation of each type of cookie we use.

Cookie settings

We use 2 types of cookie. You can choose which cookies you're happy for us to use.

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the service to work and for us to keep your information secure. It is not possible to decline the use of these cookies.

We use this data to:

  • remember your progress through the service
  • prevent certain types of hacking attempts
  • log you in to the service
  • store your cookie preferences

Cookies to measure performance

Some cookies are stored in your browser to allow us to measure the performance of our service. We use a service called Matomo to measure how you use this digital service. The Matomo service uses cookies to collect information about:

  • how you got to this service
  • the pages you visit and how long you spend on each page
  • what you click on while you are using this service
  • which parts of our service do and don't work well for users
